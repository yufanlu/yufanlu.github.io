About Me

Jan 22, 2018

I am Quantitative Research Analyst at ITG, Toronto, Canada.

I obtained Master of Science in Computational Finance (MSCF) at Carnegie Mellon University (class 2018). And I interned at ITG algorithmic trading team at New York. Before that I studied Computing and Financial Management (Co-op) at University of Waterloo. During my undergraduate, I co-oped/interned at Manulife Financial, Bank of Nova Scotia, and Bank of Montreal, then rejoined BMO Fixed Income Strategy team before I went to CMU.
